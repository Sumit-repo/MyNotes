Association
An entity communicating with other entity through objects is known as association
    * It is also known as has-a-relationship
    * Association is of two types.
        1. Aggrigation
            * Declaring the ref of one class inside another class is known as Aggrigation.
            * The existence of a container object is not completely dependent on the existence of contained object.
        2. Composition
            * Composition is a type of Aggrigation.
            * The existence of the container object is completely dependent on the existence of contained object.
